Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

I'm currently based in Sheffield which has more trees per head of population than any other European city and is quoted as England's "greenest city". My career began in commercial kitchens but after 15 years as a professional chef I wanted to expand my skill set and had become increasingly interested in the relationship between food production, purchasing, supply and the evolving Tech industry. After a couple of start-up projects that fused food and Tech I wanted to move beyond working with developers as simply the "food expert" and wanted to get involved more with the Tech side.

I had become fascinated looking over the shoulders of colleagues at the mysterious world of code and seeing it merge as tangible digital products and interfaces. It has driven me towards a path to full-stack development. I've found that the discipline and methodology required to produce high level food is very similar to the skills and philosophy of clean, functional and well written code. I now find myself with digital skills complemented by a deep understanding of tangible industries. It empowers me to offer a complete service to clients and help them to find solutions to a range of business needs.
